About us

We work around Quantum

Quantum Computing Group IIT Roorkee, founded in 2020, is a part of ACM IIT Roorkee Student Chapter. Our members have varied interests and skills in different domains of quantum computing and attempt to elevate the technological standing of the campus populace in those specializations. We regularly hold discussions and have launched several open projects for them to spread the culture of quantum computing and integrate people interested in this field.
